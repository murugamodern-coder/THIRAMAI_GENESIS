# Agro-Industrial Baselines

## Jaggery Plant
- Baseline crushing and boiling throughput target: 25 tons cane per day.
- Typical yield band: 9.5% to 11.5% jaggery recovery by weight.
- Preferred workflow: cane receipt -> cleaning -> crushing -> clarification -> evaporation -> molding.

## Biomass Energy
- Primary fuel options: bagasse, agri-residue briquettes, and firewood blend.
- Operating approach: biomass boiler for thermal load with grid as backup for motors.
- Risk control: maintain one-week biomass buffer and moisture-controlled storage.

## Drip Irrigation
- Suitable for sugarcane and intercrops under constrained water profile.
- Trigger irrigation around soil moisture threshold 0.30 (tunable by season).
- Integrate fertigation schedule per crop stage for better water-use efficiency.
